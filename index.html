Peddie Computer Science Club

Founded in 2018, the Peddie Computer Science Club's mission is to create an environment where students can interact with others who either love computer science or are just starting to develop their passion for it. To foster creativity and enthusiasm for the fields of Computer Science and STEM, the club hosts a wide range of activities each term. The club events include seminars from experts in STEM, Hackathons, and both long-term and short-term programming projects.
